§ 01 — The idea

A single luminous line
moving through darkness.

The site opens on a single line — a life. At a marked point, an infection. The line splits. One branch stays bright; the other dims and drifts. The camera pulls back: thousands of lines, a galaxy of lives, each with its dimmed branch.

The disease, shown at population scale, in one camera move. The tagline lands as physics — the past is stolen, the future is not.

§ 02 — The mechanic

Ignite your branch.

Ahead of every dimmed line lies a fan of dormant futures: the worlds where treatment exists. Every donation ignites one.

  • Bright line— the life lived
  • Stolen branch— the world that was taken, in empathy beige
  • Ignited future— the world you make possible, in blue
  • Milestone— a research shift, in lime
